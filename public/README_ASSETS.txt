This project includes simple placeholder pixel assets (public/assets).

To use your own character or tiles:
- Replace public/assets/player.png with your sprite sheet.
  * Frame size used: 36x48 (change in public/game.js if different)
  * Frames: 0 idle, 1-4 run, 5 jump
- Replace public/assets/tiles.png with your tileset (tile size 32x32)
- Replace public/assets/enemy.png, coin.png, flag.png as desired
